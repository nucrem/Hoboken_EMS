About HVAC

Welcome to the Hoboken Volunteer Ambulance Corps official website. On behalf of the administration and the membership, we thank you for your interest in our organization.

HVAC is the last all volunteer ambulance service in Hudson County, NJ. Responding to the Hoboken Police call sign "134", we respond to over 5,000 calls a year. No one has ever received a bill for our services.

We are always looking for highly motivated volunteers to join us, so if you are interested you can apply at any time. You may apply online or find paper applications at our base, located at 707 Clinton Street in Hoboken, NJ.
