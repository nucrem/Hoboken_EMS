The Hoboken Volunteer Ambulance Corps is committed to providing the best level of pre-hospital care to the city of Hoboken. Our membership understands how important it is to continue their education as Emergency Medical Service providers.

We are proud of our classroom on the 3rd floor of our facility, which is used for monthly meetings and education.

Our educational courses vary from certifying the general public in performing Cardio Pulmonary Resuscitation (CPR) to continuing education for our Emergency Medical Technicians. Our training is brought to you by state certified instructors as well as our own membership.

Please see below for our class calendar. Depending on classroom availability, these opportunities are open to other organizations by simply contacting the Training Lieutenant at hvac.traininglt@gmail.com

All classes will take place at our headquarters, located at 707 Clinton Street in Hoboken, NJ. You will be notified of any location changes.
